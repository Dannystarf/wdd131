Nigeria

Nigeria is a vibrant and diverse country in West Africa. It is home to over 2.6% of the world's population and is Africa’s most populous nation. Nigeria is rich in cultural heritage, with over 500 ethnic groups and more than 500 languages spoken across the country. The nation is best known for its vibrant music and film industries — including Afrobeats and Nollywood, the second-largest film industry in the world. It is also known for its natural resources, especially oil and gas, as well as its bustling cities like Lagos and Abuja, and rich traditions in art, fashion, and cuisine.
